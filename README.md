# Sumorrow (Summit Tomorrow)

Sumorrow is a one-stop digital platform designed specifically for mountain hikers in Indonesia. The name "Sumorrow" reflects optimism and preparation for reaching the summit in the future.

Today, hiking information in Indonesia is still scattered across different platforms and not integrated into a complete system. As a result, hikers often struggle to access important details such as real-time weather conditions, mountain status, and well-structured trip documentation.

This platform is not only a mountain information directory, but also a personal assistant for hikers. Sumorrow integrates real-time weather data from OpenWeatherMap and mountain activity status from relevant authorities (such as PVMBG) to improve hiking safety. In addition, the platform allows users to document their trips through an interactive Hiking Log and manage equipment through a Digital Gear List.

Sumorrow is expected to become an integrated solution that helps hikers plan, carry out, and document hiking activities more safely and efficiently.

## Target users

- **Beginner Hikers:** users who need reliable information on mountain difficulty, estimated hiking time, official routes, and gear recommendations so their first hikes are safer and better planned.
- **Experienced Hikers (Enthusiast/Expert):** users who want to document their hiking history (Hiking Log), pursue milestones (Achievement Badge), and use technical tools such as a Weight Calculator to manage backpack load.
- **Community and Contributors:** hikers who actively share updates about trail conditions, provide reviews, and contribute new mountain data through a submission system.
